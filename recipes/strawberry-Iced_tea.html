Starwberry Iced Tea

Description

This strawberry iced tea, cool and refreshing, is flavored with fresh strawberries. I like to leave sliced strawberries in my tea, but you can also blend the strawberries and add the smooth mixture to the tea.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 4 bags black tea
  • 1 (16 ounce) container fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon strawberry syrup, such as Torani
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice

Directions

  1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Remove from heat and add tea bags. Let steep for 1 hour; remove tea bags.
  2. Place strawberries in a large bowl and sprinkle with sugar. Let stand 10 minutes; stir. Stir in strawberry syrup and lemon juice. Mash strawberries to desired consistency
  3. Pour strawberry mixture, tea, and 2 cups cold water into a pitcher. Stir well. Chill until ready to use.
